Welcome to Our Hospital

At Multi-Speciality Hospital, we provide world-class healthcare services across various super specialities. Our team of expert doctors and state-of-the-art facilities ensure the best outcomes for our patients. We are committed to delivering compassionate care with the latest medical technology.

Explore our specialities, facilities, and more to learn how we can help you.

Super Specialities

Cardiology

Advanced heart care including angioplasty, bypass surgery, and cardiac rehabilitation. Our cardiologists use cutting-edge technology for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

Neurology

Treatment for brain and nervous system disorders, including stroke management and epilepsy care. We offer comprehensive neurological assessments and therapies.

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care with chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology. Our oncology team provides personalized treatment plans.

Orthopedics

Joint replacements, spine surgery, and sports injury treatments. We specialize in minimally invasive procedures for faster recovery.

Gastroenterology

Digestive health services including endoscopy and liver disease management. Our experts handle a wide range of gastrointestinal issues.

Nephrology

Kidney care with dialysis and transplant services. We provide state-of-the-art renal care for chronic and acute conditions.

Urology

Treatment for urinary tract issues, including prostate surgery and kidney stone removal. Our urologists offer advanced surgical options.

Endocrinology

Management of diabetes, thyroid disorders, and hormonal imbalances. We focus on holistic endocrine health.

Pulmonology

Respiratory care for asthma, COPD, and lung infections. Our pulmonologists provide comprehensive lung health services.

Dermatology

Skin care services including cosmetic dermatology and treatment for skin conditions. We offer both medical and aesthetic dermatology.

Facilities Available

  • Emergency Department: 24/7 emergency care with rapid response teams.
  • Intensive Care Units (ICUs): Specialized ICUs for cardiac, neuro, and general critical care.
  • Operation Theaters: Modern OT suites equipped with advanced surgical technology.
  • Diagnostic Labs: In-house labs for blood tests, imaging (MRI, CT, X-ray), and pathology.
  • Pharmacy: 24-hour pharmacy with a wide range of medications.
  • Ambulance Services: Fleet of ambulances for patient transport.
  • Patient Rooms: Comfortable private, semi-private, and general wards.
  • Rehabilitation Center: Physiotherapy and occupational therapy services.
  • Cafeteria: Nutritious meals and dietary counseling.
  • Parking and Accessibility: Ample parking and wheelchair-accessible facilities.
